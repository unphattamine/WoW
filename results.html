Background

NICE offers a cloud-based customer experience platform equipped with a comprehensive suite of tools for managing contact center interactions. SS&C aims to leverage Autopilot, integrated with TA2000 Server, to automate call flows based on specific use cases provided by SS&C. This proposal outlines how we will develop Autopilot to enhance customer experience through automation. Autopilot facilitates conversational AI-driven, customer intent-based routing.
